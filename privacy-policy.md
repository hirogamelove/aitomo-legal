# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-22

This privacy policy explains how AI Tomo Connection handles user data.

## Data We Collect

The app may collect or store the following data:

- User profile data such as display name, MBTI, age, gender, occupation, hobby, personality, speech style, and profile text
- AI Tomo profile data such as name, MBTI, image, profile text, personality, and relationship state
- Chat messages, group chat messages, relationship status, affinity scores, and game progress
- Purchase information such as product ID, purchase token, transaction ID, and subscription status
- Anonymous device identifier used to create and maintain an account
- Push notification token when notifications are enabled
- Technical logs needed to operate and debug the service

## How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Provide individual chats, group chats, AI Tomo relationships, notifications, and game features
- Generate AI replies using profile, relationship, and recent chat context
- Verify purchases and apply paid benefits
- Prevent abuse and excessive use
- Improve app quality and investigate issues

## Third-Party Services

The app may send necessary data to the following services:

- OpenAI: to generate AI chat replies and related text
- Google Play: to verify Android purchases and subscriptions
- Expo: to deliver push notifications and support app builds
- Hosting provider: to run the backend server

Only the data needed for each feature is sent.

## Images

Images selected by the user may be stored locally or in app/server storage for AI Tomo icons. Video generation and background removal are disabled in the current release.

## Purchases

Payment information such as credit card numbers is handled by Google Play or other payment providers. The app receives only purchase verification data and entitlement status.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete AI Tomo data in the app. Full account deletion can be requested through the support contact below until an in-app account deletion flow is added.

## Children

The app is not intended for children who cannot use the service without guardian consent under applicable law.
